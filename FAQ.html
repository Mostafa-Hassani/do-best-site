Moving Services

What areas do you provide moving services in?

We offer moving services in Helsinki, Espoo, and Vantaa, and we can also handle long-distance moves anywhere in Finland or the Schengen area, as long as the starting point or destination is one of these three cities.

Do you offer packing and unpacking services?

Yes! Our team provides professional packing and unpacking services to ensure your belongings are safe during transport. We use high-quality packing materials and handle everything from fragile items to large furniture.

Can you help with office relocations?

Absolutely! We specialize in office moving services and can assist with packing, disassembling office furniture, and setting up your new space efficiently.

Are my belongings insured during the move?

Yes, all moves are covered by basic insurance. Home insurance plays a crucial role in safeguarding your belongings during a move. It is recommended to ensure that your home insurance policy includes coverage for damages that may occur during packing, carrying, and moving. Additionally, road transport insurance provides coverage for damages during transit, with compensation typically capped at €20/kg. We advise maintaining comprehensive home insurance to cover any unforeseen incidents during the move, with the assurance that we will assist with the insurance deductible in the event of an accident. Cleaning Services

What's included in a move-out cleaning?

Our move-out cleaning covers everything needed to get your apartment or house ready for the next tenant or inspection. It includes: Deep cleaning of all rooms, Kitchen and bathroom sanitation, Window cleaning, Floor washing.
