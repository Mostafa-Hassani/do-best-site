About Do Best

Trusted Moving & Cleaning Services in Helsinki, Espoo & Vantaa

Welcome to Do Best, where professional moving and cleaning services meet reliability and excellence. Founded in December 2022, we've quickly built a reputation as one of the most trusted moving and cleaning companies in Helsinki, Espoo, and Vantaa, delivering 100% customer satisfaction with every service.

At Do Best, we understand that moving can be overwhelming and cleaning can be time-consuming. Whether you're relocating to a new home, moving your office, or need a deep cleaning for your space, our expert team is here to make the process smooth, fast, and stress-free.

Our Services

  • House & Office Moving – Local & long-distance moves across Finland and the Schengen area (with Helsinki, Espoo, or Vantaa as the starting point or destination).
  • Deep Cleaning & Move-Out Cleaning – Eco-friendly and thorough cleaning for homes, offices, and rental apartments.
  • Furniture Assembly & Appliance Installation – Fast and precise assembling of flat-pack furniture and installation/removal of appliances.
